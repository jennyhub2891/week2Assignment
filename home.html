Your pooch becoming your well behaved best friend is our goal.

Why train your dog?

Dogs can be a great addition to a family. However just like with people dogs have differing personalities and needs. In order to create the best environment for you and your dog, training is important.

How we train:

Understanding that everyone has a different learning style, we offer several methods of training for dogs and their owners.

  • Access to Training videos
  • Weekly hands on class training
  • Board and train
  • Come to your home weekly and train
